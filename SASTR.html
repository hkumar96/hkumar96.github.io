SASTR

Semi-Autonomous Surveillance and Transportation Robot

What it does!

Our robot can perform autonomous navigation and transportation using GPS and Google maps data. Given a final point it would travel autonomously from its location to final point. It should avoid obstacles. It should do surveillance.

How was it made!

The work to be done was divided into three teams

Mechanical

The work of mechanical team was to design a robot that can move on rough terrain. After discussion it was decided that we will make a robot that has a design similar to that of the tank.

Electronics

This team had to ensure that correct components of required specification were bought. They assembled the components in the robot and ensured every component was placed and connected properly.

Programming

Programming team was responsible for planning the algorithm of the movement of the robot, implementing the filters and design a program that could process Google maps data and GPS data from robot.
